STAKEHOLDER GOALS & OBJECTIVES
•Mobile and Web applications are not successful by random events or by chance. Only when a new release offers it’s consumers a purchase that makes sense will it find the success and popularity that Apple Inc. thrives off as a winning competitor. As the long running leaders in the industry, their stakes are exceptionally high, but as a result, their claims are extraordinary. With goals to continue dominating the world of information, one of Apple’s main objectives is to not only hold on to, but to strengthen their reputation within the market.
•
•The following stakeholder goals and objectives will guide the strategic development of the “Laundri-Map Pocket Laundry Assistant” mobile application:
•
ÿThe intention for the product is a mobile app that will be equally functional on iPhone and iPad devices and will integrate many rich and interactive features designed to make life a little easier. All information used comes from authoritative experts who have gained their insight through a variety of industries that deal with fashion/home care.
ÿ
ÿLifetime subscriptions are obtained with the small initial purchase price. The app will likely gain popularity within the broader market of iPhone and iPad users through word-of-mouth.
ÿ
ÿThe concept for the “Laundri-Map Pocket Laundry Assistant” is to serve as a guide and calculator for sorting and managing one’s personal laundry loads, according to proven guidelines and formulated settings for grouping items of laundry in various washing and drying cycles.
ÿ
ÿThe app is programmed to compute and offer back specific directions based on input from the users. The data is used to inventory, describe, tag and catalog their own items of clothing within an indexed database containing categorized information to identify each item. This “inventory” of clothing is visually displayed in a gallery within the app -- viewable with thumbnail images.
