THE BASIC STEPS TO CLEAN LAUNDRY

...In most cases, they're not so black-and-white.

Colors can bleed or fade in the wash to wreak havoc on clothes that no amount of rewashing can undo. Different patterns, prints, fabric contents and delicate garment embellishments are further complicating factors when it comes to washer load compatibility.

While it may be easy to keep your "like colors" in groups, the more perplexing attributes to sort out may be "lightness", "brightness", and "dinginess" of colors. Further complicating these subjective criteria are the water temperature settings that correspond to different categories of laundry colors and fabrics.

The Load Wizard laundry sorting engine was developed for use inside the laundry room or laundromat from your smartphone or tablet. The screens that follow will allow you to create lists of dirty laundry, with the name and snapshot of each item recorded with a simple color and fabric profile that remains stored in your Closet Galleries album. You can edit and save Laundry Lists, then just click to sort all of your scheduled items into compatible washer loads!

The Load Wizard is programmed to select the recommended water temperature settings for each item, and to calculate separate loads for each cycle level that are grouped for compatibility of colors, product types, and fabric properties among efficiently grouped laundry items. The Sorted Loads preview window displays a collapsible table array for each scheduled load, with names and thumbnail photos of the included laundry items listed under the heading for the advised water temperature and detergent type.

The following interactive screens provide easy menu options and step-by-step instructions for you to clean and care for your garments more effectively, while minimizing the risks of clothing damage...with virtually no more effort or "Home Ec." training required.

HOW TO USE THE LOAD WIZARD

  1. At the top of this screen: Compile a Laundry List showing all dirty laundry items to be sorted for washing. Click to create New List. (Or, click on the Open Saved... button to select a previously stored list).
  2. In the New Laundry List screen: Enter a List Name. Add items to the list, by clicking on Create New Entry to input and store data for profiling new laundry items. And/or browse and retrieve previous laundry item records that have been stored within your virtual closet, by clicking on the Get Laundry From Closet Galleries... button.
  3. Within the Create New Entry window: Input an Item Name, take a photo of your new laundry item from your built-in camera, and choose a gallery section of your virtual closet for saving profile information for the item. Complete fields as possible to define textile, fabric and optional other attributes, and click on a swatch in the color palette to match the main color of the item. Then, click the Save and Add to List button to store the item data in Closet Galleries and insert into your open Laundry List.
  4. After you've entered all list items inside the New Laundry List screen, click on Save and Preview. If you still wish to add or delete items, click on the EDIT LIST button. When you are ready to sort your Laundry List contents into loads for the wash, click on the SORT LOADS button.
  5. View your Sorted Loads table in the popup window by clicking the panels to display or collapse items listed for each of the cycles and temperature levels. (Click to Close and return to the Preview Laundry List screen.)
